about our school

And our learning model

Our curriculum combines a modern approach to education with traditional teaching methods.

Through this innovative concept, we strive to develop the intellectual, social and personal aspects of our students' lives. We are able to achieve this by using the very best learning resources, together with an experienced team of educators and an unconventional learning environment. Our personalised learning model focuses on learning beyond the classroom, ensuring that our students are prepared and equipped for life after school.
This multi-disciplinary approach encourages our students to breach the barriers of a blinkered approach to life with an open mind, enthusiuasm, a desire to solve problems and the ability to take calculated risks in our ever-changing world.

our approach

And the education system

We are all expected to keep up with the rapid pace at which the world around us changes, but our education system doesn't always provide the necessary tools to do so.

In today's competitive climate, a secondary school education focused only on cognitive development no longer suffices. Our belief is that education needs to adapt in the same way we do.
Our approach to preparing our students for life after school is a holistic one, emphasising personal development, career readiness and an ability to problem-solve in any environment.

5 reasons to choose us

And get a headstart

According to the department of education 439 764 students received a National Senior Certificate last year. In today's competitive climate, a secondary school education focusing only on cognitive development no longer suffices. We help our students establish a personal brand and coach them to develop an extensive set of character skills, setting them apart from the crowd.

Unlimited career choices after graduation

Traditional schooling allows students to disqualify themselves for many of the most interesting and sought-after university degree courses at an early age through their choice of elective subjects. At M&M Academy, we ensure that all our graduates meet the requirements for a variety of degree courses so that they are not limited in their choice by decisions about subject choices taken at a young age.

Accomodation of personalised schedules

We teach our learners in very small groups, which enables us to provide more comprehensive and more personalised guidance than traditional group sizes would permit. Instead of assigning the same homework to the entire group, our educators personalise homework assignments to ideally challenge each learner as an individual. Where required, extensive 1:1 tutoring is available outside of the classroom.

Education beyond the CAPS Curriculum

Success in tertiary education and ultimately in labour markets now required high levels of technological competence. We have incorporated basic document processing, data analysis, and elementary coding into our curriculum. They are taught 'from the ground up' with no previous knowledge required of learners. By the time they graduate, our students have obtained a practical skillset not developed in traditional secondary education – in addition to excellent training in academic subjects

Personal and unconventional learning environment

Educators at M&M Academy foster a relationship with learners that we like to refer to as 'informal but respectful.' We believe that learning new concepts – if done right – can be a highly stimulating experience. One of the many ways in which we pursue our mission to make our learners rediscover the joy in learning is to cultivate a climate where they can be themselves and are appreciated for their unique personal characteristics – a climate that appreciates diversity.

Scholarships

Our scholarships are entirely based on merit and reflect exceptional ability or potential in the following categories: Academic, Music, Sport, Art, Drama, Design and Technology. The levels of scholarships are fixed and are based on performance and merit. We offer Gold, Silver, and Bronze scholarships. A Gold scholarship corresponds to a 80% reduction in tuition fees. A Silver scholarship corresponds to a 50% reduction in tuition fees. A Bronze scholarship grants a 25% reduction. Candidates do not have to be registered with the school prior to entering the scholarship process but are also available to students already enrolled.

Bursaries

At Montgomery & Miller Academy, we believe that a learner's opportunities to attain to her fullest potential should never be limited by financial circumstances. A limited number of means-tested bursaries are available to families in need of additional financial support and may, if required, supplement scholarships. There is no fixed rate and awards are based entirely on individual family circumstances and the limit of funds available. Bursaries are also available to families with children already in the school who are up to date with the payment of fees, but who now find that their financial circumstances have changed. Please contact our Management directly for further details.
